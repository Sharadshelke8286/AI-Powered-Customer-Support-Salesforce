# Stakeholder Mapping

## 📌 Overview
Stakeholder mapping identifies the key individuals and groups involved in or impacted by the AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation project.

---

## 🔹 Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Project Sponsor
- Defines business objectives
- Approves deliverables
- Ensures alignment with organizational goals
- Influence Level: High
- Interest Level: High

### 2. Salesforce System Administrator
- Configures Salesforce objects
- Implements Prompt Builder and Agentforce AI features
- Manages user roles and security
- Influence Level: High
- Interest Level: High

### 3. Support Agents
- Create and manage customer cases
- Use AI-generated responses and summaries
- Ensure timely issue resolution
- Influence Level: Medium
- Interest Level: High

---

## 🔹 Secondary Stakeholders

### 4. Support Team
- Reviews case information
- Provides additional customer assistance
- Influence Level: Medium
- Interest Level: Medium

### 5. Management
- Monitors dashboards and reports
- Analyzes performance trends
- Supports decision-making
- Influence Level: High
- Interest Level: High

### 6. IT Support
- Maintains system performance
- Resolves technical issues
- Ensures system reliability
- Influence Level: Medium
- Interest Level: Medium

---

## 📊 Influence–Interest Matrix

| Stakeholder | Interest | Influence |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Project Sponsor | High | High |
| Salesforce Admin | High | High |
| Support Agents | High | Medium |
| Management | High | High |
| IT Support | Medium | Medium |

---

## ✅ Conclusion
Effective stakeholder engagement ensures successful implementation of AI-powered automation within the Salesforce platform, improving operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
