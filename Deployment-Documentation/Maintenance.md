# Maintenance, Monitoring & Troubleshooting

## Overview
Maintenance, Monitoring, and Troubleshooting ensures that the AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation system continues to operate efficiently after deployment.

## Maintenance
Regular maintenance activities include reviewing Salesforce automation such as:
- Record Triggered Flows
- Validation Rules
- Case Assignment Queues
- AI Prompt Templates

Administrators periodically update configurations based on changing business requirements and system updates.

## Monitoring
System monitoring helps identify performance issues and automation failures.

Monitoring activities include:
- Checking Flow execution logs
- Reviewing Case assignment accuracy
- Monitoring AI generated responses
- Tracking user activity through reports and dashboards

Salesforce error notifications and logs help administrators detect problems early.

## Troubleshooting
Troubleshooting involves identifying and resolving system issues such as:

- Failed automation flows
- Incorrect case routing
- AI summary generation errors
- Permission and access problems

Issues are first tested in a sandbox environment and then safely deployed to production after validation.
